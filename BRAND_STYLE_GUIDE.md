# Verdent Brand Style Guide

## Brand Positioning

**Verdent** is a professional third-party coordination service for incentive-supported clean equipment purchases. The brand should communicate:

- **Professionalism**: Serious, reliable, operations-focused
- **Trustworthiness**: Credible, transparent, organized
- **Competence**: Expert, systematic, detail-oriented
- **Collaboration**: Supportive, facilitative, non-competitive

## Voice & Tone

### Writing Style

- **Plain English**: Avoid jargon unless explained
- **Professional**: Business-appropriate, respectful
- **Specific**: Concrete details over vague claims
- **Honest**: No overselling or exaggeration
- **Clear**: Simple sentence structure

### Things to Avoid

- Em dashes (use commas, periods, or parentheses instead)
- Startup jargon ("disrupt", "synergy", "leverage")
- Hyperbolic claims ("best", "revolutionary", "game-changing")
- Vague promises ("seamless", "end-to-end", "turnkey")
- Implying Verdent is the incentive program or approves vouchers

### Example Phrases

**Good:**
- "Verdent helps coordinate..."
- "We support the documentation process..."
- "Verdent works alongside approved dealers..."
- "We help organize paperwork..."

**Avoid:**
- "Verdent gets you approved..."
- "We guarantee incentive savings..."
- "Verdent submits all vouchers..."
- "We control the process..."

## Typography

### Font Family

**Inter** - A clean, professional sans-serif with excellent readability.

```css
font-family: 'Inter', system-ui, sans-serif;
```

### Font Scale

| Name | Size | Weight | Usage |

## Spacing

Use an 8px grid system for consistent spacing:

- **4px** (0.25rem): Tight spacing
- **8px** (0.5rem): Small gaps
- **16px** (1rem): Standard gaps
- **24px** (1.5rem): Medium sections
- **32px** (2rem): Large sections
- **48px** (3rem): Section padding (mobile)
- **64px** (4rem): Section padding (desktop)
- **96px** (6rem): Major section breaks

## Components

### Buttons

**Primary**: Forest green background, white text
- Hover: Slightly lighter green
- Use for: Main CTAs ("Contact Us", "Get Started")

**Secondary**: Amber background, white text
- Use for: Alternative CTAs, emphasis

**Outline**: Forest green border, transparent background
- Hover: Light green background
- Use for: Secondary actions, learn more links

### Cards

- White background
- Subtle border (slate-100)
- Rounded corners (12px/16px)
- Soft shadow on hover
- Generous padding (24px-32px)

### Forms

- Clean, minimal styling
- Clear labels above fields
- Visible focus states (green ring)
- Error states in red with helpful messages
- Success confirmation with green checkmark

## Imagery Guidelines

### Photography

When adding photos:
- Authentic business/industrial settings
- Real equipment and work environments
- Diverse, professional people
- Natural lighting preferred
- Avoid generic stock photo clichés

### Icons

- Simple, line-based icons
- Consistent 1.5px stroke weight
- Match the current text color
- 24px standard size, 20px-32px range

### Logo

The logo consists of:
- Shield icon with checkmark (trust + verification)
- "Verdent" wordmark in Inter semibold
- Forest green color (#1B4332)

## Layout Principles

### Whitespace

Use generous whitespace. Empty space communicates professionalism and clarity.

### Hierarchy

1. Clear visual hierarchy through size and weight
2. Most important information first
3. Progressive disclosure for complex topics

### Alignment

- Left-align body text
- Center-align hero sections and CTAs
- Consistent margins and gutters

### Responsive Design

- Mobile-first approach
- Breakpoints: 640px, 768px, 1024px, 1280px
- Touch-friendly tap targets (44px minimum)
- Readable text sizes on all devices

## Trust Signals

Always include visual trust signals:

- Years of experience
- Transactions supported
- Response time commitment
- Service area
- Contact information
- LinkedIn presence
- Clear disclaimers about Verdent's role

## Legal Disclaimers

Every page mentioning incentive programs should include appropriate disclaimers:

> Verdent is an independent third-party coordination service. We are not affiliated with any government incentive program, and we do not approve, guarantee, or award incentives.

Place this in:
- Footer (abbreviated)
- California CORE page (prominent)
- Any page discussing incentives
